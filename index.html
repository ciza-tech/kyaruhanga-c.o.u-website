About Our Church

KYARUHANGA CHURCH OF UGANDA is located opposite Alliance Secondary School in Ibanda District, Uganda. Our church exists to glorify God through worship, prayer, teaching the Word of God and serving the community. We welcome everyone to worship with us every Sunday.

Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ through evangelism, worship, fellowship and service.

Vision

A Christ-centered church transforming lives, families and communities through the Gospel.
